# 🔰 Operators — Interview Questions and Answers

---

## 1️⃣ **What are operators in Java?**

**A:** Operators are symbols used to perform operations on variables and values.

---

## 2️⃣ **What are the types of operators in Java?**

**A:** Arithmetic, relational, logical, assignment, bitwise, unary, and ternary operators.

---

## 3️⃣ **Difference between = and == ?**

**A:** = is an assignment operator used to assign values, while == is a comparison operator used to compare values.

---

## 4️⃣ **What is integer division?**

**A:** Integer division is the division of two integers that results in an integer value by discarding the decimal part.

---

## 5️⃣ **Difference between pre-increment and post-increment?**

**A:** Pre-increment (++i) increases the value before use, while post-increment (i++) increases the value after use.

---

## 6️⃣ **What is short-circuit evaluation?**

**A:** Short-circuit evaluation means that in logical operations (&&, ||), evaluation stops as soon as the result is determined.

---

## 7️⃣ **What is ternary operator?**

**A:** The ternary operator is a shorthand for if-else: condition ? value1 : value2.

---

## 8️⃣ **What are bitwise operators?**

**A:** Bitwise operators perform operations on individual bits, such as &, |, ^, ~, <<, >>.

---

## 9️⃣ **What is instanceof operator?**

**A:** instanceof is used to check whether an object is an instance of a specific class or interface.

---

## 🔟 **What is operator precedence?**

**A:** Operator precedence defines the order in which operations are performed in an expression.

---

## 1️⃣1️⃣ **Difference between & and && ?**

**A:** & evaluates both operands, while && uses short-circuit evaluation and may skip evaluating the second operand.

---

## 1️⃣2️⃣ **Why float comparison fails sometimes?**

**A:** Float comparison fails due to precision errors in floating-point representation.

---

## 1️⃣3️⃣ **What are compound operators?**

**A:** Compound operators combine arithmetic and assignment operations, such as +=, -=, *=, /=.

---

## 1️⃣4️⃣ **Common mistakes in operators?**

**A:** Using = instead of ==, ignoring operator precedence, and incorrect use of logical operators.

---

## 1️⃣5️⃣ **Why avoid using String + in loops?**

**A:** Because it creates multiple string objects and reduces performance; StringBuilder is preferred.

---
